ATTRIBUTES
STR
Strength
Your ability to perform physical tasks when trying to physically exert and overpower an obstacle uses Strength. Strength is one of the primary attributes used for those who possess great melee prowess.
END
Endurance
Your ability to take punishment after your Aura has been broken and how long you can perform any task that requires physical exertion. Withstanding hazardous environmental and physical trauma is also a key aspect of those with high Endurance. Additionally, Endurance determines a Character’s Health Points.
AGI
Agility
Your ability to move quickly, precisely, and with poise. Tasks that require accurate and precise movement or use of delicate maneuvers require Agility. Agility is one of the primary attributes used by those excelling in ranged combat. It also affects your movement bonus.
PER
Perception
Your ability to defend yourself relies on your Perception. Perception is used to spot weaknesses or incoming attacks to better defend oneself as well as hone in on your own intuition about others.
WIL
Willpower
Your ability to Influence others and manipulate your Semblance. You also have a knack for understanding how to approach aggressive and delicate social interactions.
DIS
Discipline
Your ability to be studious and keep calm during the heat of battle. Characters with high Discipline exhibit extreme form and finesse when wielding Dust. Individuals with high Discipline are highly skilled with Dust without applying it to a weapon.
MOVE
Movement
The amount of distance you can travel with one Action.
The default distance you move is 15ft. Gain +5ft for every 4 AGI.

RESOURCES
HP
HP is equal to your END + 1. How much damage a Character can withstand without their Aura before becoming Defeated. Health Points are commonly referred to as HP. When your HP is 0, your Character is considered Defeated. Only HP restoration can bring a Character out of the Defeated state.
/ 
ARMOR
Some Characters wear extra Armor, or a Faunus may have natural Armor. Reduce any damage that would be directed to your HP by 1 for every point of Armor you possess to a minimum of 1. The maximum Armor you may ever have is equal to 1 + 1 for every 4 points of Strength you possess.
/ 
AURA
Aura is equal to your WIL + DIS + 2. The physical manifestation of a Character’s soul. Every Huntsman and Huntress is trained in its capabilities. Aura acts as a barrier to protect your body from damage. When your Aura is 0 your Character cannot use their Semblance; any damage inflicted will be directed to your HP.
/ 
CAP
The amount of ammunition left in a magazine, emergency supplies, or easily accessible Dust a character has at the current moment. Capacity is expended when a Character performs a ranged attack, Dust Skill Check or other Combat Action. When your Capacity is 0 your Character cannot perform Actions that require Capacity to be performed. Capacity may be restored to its maximum by using a Minor Action called Reload.
/ 
SUPPLY
Representing the amount of backup ammunition, Dust, or other essentials the Character has on their person but not readily available. Supplies are expended by 1 when a Character performs a Reload. When your Supplies are 0 your Character cannot perform a Reload.
/ 
ROC
A special resource available in Combat Encounters. Rule of Cool may be awarded by GM for a lavish and cool description of an attack, a partner attack, team attack, or anything at all they feel deserves it. The maximum RoC you can possess is 5.
/ 
DESTINY
A special Resource that is always available. It allows a re-roll of any die in the game!
DEFENSE CHECKS
Dodge
Uses your PER and AGI Attributes. When you successfully perform a Dodge Defense Check and beat the attack by at least 5, you may perform a Move action in any direction at the end of all Opposing Entities’ Turns.
Dust
Uses your PER and DIS Attributes. Reduce your Capacity by 1 when performing a Dust Defense. When you successfully perform a Dust Defense Check and beat the attack value by at least 5, you may perform the following effect based on the type of Dust you have Active.
► Red: Inflict Burning to your attacker.
► Yellow: You may gain 1 point of Armor or Cover.
► Green: You gain Cover.
► Blue: Inflict Disarmed to your attacker.
► White: Inflict Chilled to your attacker.
► Orange: Inflict Immobilized to your attacker.
► Violet: You move up to 15ft in any direction.
► Black: Inflict Blinded to your attacker.
► Brown: Reduce the attacker’s defense by 1 until the end of the Combat Encounter.
► Pink: Reduce Attacker’s attack by 1 until the end of the Combat Encounter.
Parry
Uses your PER and STR Attributes. When you successfully perform a Parry Defense Check and beat the attack by at least 5, you may inflict X Damage to your attacker where X is your Perception. You cannot Parry Ranged Attacks.
Resist
Uses your PER and END Attributes. When you fail a Resist Defense Check, you may take the damage to your HP instead of your Aura. This effect may be used multiple times per turn.
Semblance
Uses your PER and WIL Attributes. When you successfully perform a Semblance Defense Check and beat the attack value by at least 5, you may also activate your Semblance’s effect if it is not a Passive Semblance effect. You may only activate your Semblance this way once per Combat Round.
SKILL CHECKS
Carry
Uses your STR and END Attributes. The ability to carry large or heavy objects without tiring or collapsing.
Deceit
Uses your AGI and WIL Attributes. You have a knack for concealing away your true intentions, information and your own body language to make yourself hard to read.
Dust
Uses your DIS and WIL Attributes. Harnessing the power of Dust, you attempt to bend the environment to your will, adding or molding it in a manner you see fit.
Endure
Uses your END Attribute. Grrr! Being able to tough out pain to your body whether it is external or self induced from exertion.
Engineering
Uses your AGI Attribute. Careful! Working on simple or complex equipment takes a careful hand.
Expertise
Uses your DIS Attribute. You have extensive knowledge in one or more particular fields that you have read or studied in some manner.
First Aid
Uses your PER and END Attributes. Every second counts! You are able to identify and understand the limits of the physical body’s tolerances. This allows you to provide medical attention, stabalize an individual or able to assess what an individual is specifically suffering from.
Force
Uses your STR Attribute. Brute force! Use your Character’s might to attempt to destroy or remove an obstacle in your path that is preventing you from moving forward.
Grapple
Uses your STR and PER Attributes. Use a keen eye and strength to keep hold of your target.
Intimidate
Uses your STR and WIL Attributes. Attempting to use impending force or tone of voice to force another individual to be persuaded or give up assets.
Intuition
Uses your PER Attribute. It’s quiet…too quiet. Through careful study of another individual’s body language, speech or other actions, you are able to determine whether a situation feels off or if someone may be deceitful of their intentions.
Investigate
Uses your PER and DIS Attributes. What’s that? Careful observation and checking of a particular item, indivdual or location may reveal more than what is on the surface.
Negotiate
Uses your WIL and PER Attributes. Taking in the atmosphere and feeling of the situation, you are able to attempt to negotiate and diffuse a situation or convince someone of an alternative.
Performance
Uses your END and DIS Attributes. Putting your expertise to work is tough! No matter how long you are showing off to your friends or allies with your skills, it can take a toll on you.
Reflex
Uses your AGI and PER Attributes. Uh oh! Your body’s natural ability to react at a moment’s notice to danger to move out of harms way or manuever with caution both horizontally and vertically.
Resolve
Uses your END and WIL Attributes. Mind over matter! You are able to steel your nerves and mind over a long period of time against negative influences that may otherwise break other individuals.
Stealth
Uses your AGI and DIS Attributes. Shhh! You make adjustments while moving quickly, making sure you are unheard and unoticed with the utmost care and precision of movement through practice.

COMBAT ACTIONS
Boost
Uses 1 Capacity to increase an attack roll or ranged attack roll by 1D3. This may be used after seeing the result of your attack roll.
Charge Attack
Allocate how much Capacity to use in a Charge Attack.
Initiative
Uses your PER and AGI Attributes. You are ready to fight!
Thrown Melee Weapon Attack
Uses your STR + PER Attributes. You may use a Major Action to perform a Thrown Melee Weapon Attack against a Target within Medium range. If you are Disarmed, you may not perform this attack. A Thrown Melee Weapon Attack is performed by using 2D10 + STR + AGI. After performing a Thrown Melee Weapon Attack, you gain the status Disarmed. You must pickup your weapon at the Target’s location to use it again.
Thrown Melee Weapon Damage
Thrown Melee Weapon Damage is 1d6 + STR - (1 Per 5ft past Adjacent range)
Unarmed Attack
Uses your STR + AGI Attributes. You may only perform Unarmed attacks when you are under the effect of disarmed. You may perform Unarmed attacks at any time.
Unarmed Damage
Unarmed Damage is 1d6
ACTIVE DUST
Black
Black (Light)

Melee: Your Melee Attacks that hit inflict Blinded to your Target. If any Natural D10 rolled when you perform a Melee Attack is a 10, gain Advantage with all Defense Checks against Entities within Adjacent range that attack you.

Ranged: When you perform a Ranged Attack, you may gain the Barrier Status Effect. Instead of performing a normal Ranged Attack, you may Target a friendly Entity and provide them with Barrier in addition to yourself. No Ranged Attack roll is required, but a Major Action is still used in addition to the Capacity cost.
Blue
Blue (Water)

Melee: Melee Attacks while using Blue Dust are increased by 1 range increment.

Ranged: If your Target currently has any Status Effect, your Ranged Attacks that hit inflict Confused. If any Natural D10 rolled while you perform a Ranged Attack is a 10 inflict, Confused.
Brown
Brown (Acid)

Melee: Your Melee Attacks that hit reduce the defense of your Target by 1 until the end of the Combat Encounter. (This effect can only be applied to the same Target once per Combat Round)

Ranged: Your Ranged Attacks that hit reduce the Armor of your Target by 1. If the Target has no Armor, inflict Burning instead.
Green
Green (Wind)

Melee: Gain Advantage on Dodge Defense Checks. If any Natural D10 rolled while you perform a Melee Attack is a 10, you may perform a free Melee Attack after resolving your current Melee Attack (This free Melee Attack cannot generate another free Melee Attack by using Green Dust).

Ranged: Increase the distance of your Ranged Attacks by 1 range increment and gain Advantage on your Ranged Attack.
Orange
Orange (Lightning)

Melee: Your Melee Attacks that hit allow you to chain the lightning to a secondary Target within Close range of your initial Target. The secondary Target receives half damage. Modifiers from RoC never apply to the secondary Target.

Ranged: Your Ranged Attacks that hit reduce the special defense of your Target by 1 until the end of the Combat Encounter (This effect can only be applied to the same Target once per Combat Round). If any Natural D10 rolled while you perform a Ranged Attack is a 10, inflict Immobilized.
Pink
Pink (Sonic)

Melee: Your Melee Attacks that hit reduce the attack of your Target by 1 until the end of the Combat Encounter. (This effect can only be applied to the same Target once per Combat Round)

Ranged: Performing a Blindside Attack requires 1 range increment less (Ex: Long range requirement becomes Medium range). If any Natural D10 rolled when you perform a Ranged Attack is a 10, inflict the Shaken Status Effect.
Red
Red (Fire)

Melee: Your Melee Attacks that hit inflict Burning to your Target. If your Target currently has the Burning Status Effect, increase the damage inflicted by the Burning Status Effect on your Target by 2.

Ranged: Your Ranged Attacks that hit also inflict half damage to Entities within Close range of your Target. Modifiers from RoC never apply to these additional Entities. This damage cannot effect the same Entity more than once.
Violet
Violet (Force)

Melee: Melee Attacks allow you to knock back your Target up to 30ft. If your Target would impact another Entity or solid structure, inflict an additional 3 Damage to your Target and any Entity they collide into.

Ranged: Your Ranged Attacks allow you to knock back your Target up to 10ft. If your Target would impact another Entity or solid structure, inflict an additional 2 Damage to your Target and any Entity they collide into. Whenever you fire at a Target with Violet Dust, you may move up to 10ft in the opposite direction of your Target.
White
White (Ice)

Melee: Your Melee Attacks that hit add a Frozen counter to your Target (Maximum 3). Anytime an attack that is not a Melee Attack using White Dust hits a Target with 1 or more Frozen counters, remove all Frozen counters. Inflict 2 damage to your Target for each Frozen counter removed and inflict this damage to all Close range hostile Entities. Modifiers from RoC never apply to this damage.

Ranged: Your Ranged Attacks that hit inflict Chilled. If your Target currently has the Chilled Status Effect, inflict Frostbitten instead of Chilled.
Yellow
Yellow (Earth)

Melee: Increase your Melee Attacks by 3 and reduce the Armor of your Target by 1. (This effect can only be applied to the same Target once per Combat Round)

Ranged: When you perform a Ranged Attack, you may Gain 1 Armor point or Cover. Instead of performing a normal Ranged Attack, you may Target a friendly Entity and provide them with 1 Armor point or Cover in addition to yourself. No Ranged Attack is required, but a Major Action is still used in addition to the Capacity cost.
